Desert Habitat

Our desert habitat allows visitors to get an up close look at 3 Camels and 2 Peccaries. The desert habitat is just perfect for our Camels and our Peccaries!
The camels are mostly calm and like to wander around, they are all pretty sweet! Our 2 Peccaries are not always the quietest, but they are all around amazing animals.

The Camels

The Peccaries

The camels we have were gifted to us from a near-by zoo that ended up closing down, these friendly guys needed a new home.
The names of our camels are, "Emma", "Marco", and their child that is named "Riley".

Our two Peccaries were rescued as babies, they were found together and alone. We never found the mother or the siblings; if there were any.
Although they have been in captivity their whole lives, they still keep their natural instincts
like knowing how to root up the ground for fungi, roots, and bulbs. Their names are, "Milo" and "Ivan".
